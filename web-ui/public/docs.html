Xmore Documentation

Xmore is an AI-powered stock prediction system for the Egyptian Exchange (EGX) and global markets. This page documents every feature available on the dashboard and Xmore Pro.

Overview

Live

Xmore aggregates signals from 5 AI agents — Moving Average, RSI, Volume Spike, Random Forest ML, and Gemini LLM — into a unified consensus score. The dashboard auto-refreshes data on every EGX trading day.

5 AI Agents

MA Crossover, RSI, Volume Spike, Random Forest, and Gemini LLM each vote independently with weighted confidence.

Xmore Score

0–100 composite bullishness score. ≥65 = strong buy zone; ≤35 = bearish. Updated daily after market close.

Bilingual

Full English / Arabic toggle. All labels, signals, and dates switch language. Arabic uses RTL layout.

Dark / Light theme

Theme preference saved to localStorage and persists across sessions.

Responsive

Mobile-optimised layout. Tables scroll horizontally on small screens. All modals are touch-friendly.

Auth

Sign Up / Sign In with email. JWT stored in HTTP-only cookie. Portfolio, alerts, and forecasts are per-user.

Quick start
Visit xmore-project.onrender.com → sign up for free → add stocks to your watchlist → check the Predictions tab each morning before market open (09:00 Cairo).

How to read a signal row

  1. Signal — BUY means majority of agents predict price will rise within 5 trading days.
  2. Confidence — weighted average certainty across agreeing agents (0–100%). Higher = more conviction.
  3. Xmore Score — bull pressure index. Score of 80 means 80% of composite weight is bullish.
  4. Agents column — e.g. "4/5" = 4 of 5 agents voted for the consensus direction.
  5. Price — last closing price in EGP from EGX or TradingView Scanner.

Execution Realism Layer

Signal Gate

Every BUY signal passes through a friction-aware gate before it is stored. The gate models real EGX transaction costs, liquidity-based slippage, market regime, and trailing stop management — blocking signals whose edge doesn't cover their real cost.

EGX Transaction Costs

Full round-trip cost: brokerage (0.15%) + stamp duty (0.15%) + FRA fee + EGX exchange fee + Misr clearing ≈ 0.70% per trade. A signal must offer ≥ 3× this cost to be approved.

Liquidity-Tiered Slippage

High-ADV stocks (>5M EGP/day): 10 bps slippage. Mid-ADV (>1M EGP): 25 bps. Low-ADV: 60 bps. BUY fill prices are adjusted up, SELL prices adjusted down.

Market Regime Filter

EGX30 vs its 20-day MA determines regime: BULL (≥+2%), NEUTRAL (0–2%), BEAR (<0%). New long positions are blocked in BEAR regime to prevent buying into corrections.

Trailing Stop

Activates after 20 EGX trading days at 6% below the highest price since entry. The stop only ratchets up — never down. Hard exit at 45 trading days regardless of price.

Conviction Gate

BUY signals with Xmore Score < 40 (LOW conviction) are silently dropped before storage. Only MODERATE (≥40), HIGH (≥60), and VERY HIGH (≥75) BUYs enter the database — directly raising win rate and Sharpe.

HMM Regime Persistence

Each daily pipeline run fits a Gaussian Hidden Markov Model on EGX30 proxy returns and persists the result to the regime_log table. Provides a daily audit trail of market regime transitions used for regime-conditional analytics.

Investor Scoring

New

A composite score (0–1) built from 4 weighted components is translated into 6 investor-facing formats simultaneously. Switch modes in the Consensus tab to see today's signals in the format that suits your audience.

Composite Formula

Consensus × 0.40 + Execution quality × 0.25 + Market regime × 0.20 + Momentum × 0.15. Missing components default to neutral (0.5) — no data gaps penalise the score.

6 Output Modes

Score (0–100), Letter grade (A+→F), Stars (1–5 at 0.5 resolution), Signal tier (S/A/B/C/D), Conviction (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW), and Xmore native (0.00–1.00).

Mode Selector

Pill buttons in the Consensus tab let you switch between all 6 modes instantly. The table re-renders without re-fetching — no page reload needed.

Institutional Metrics

EGX-correct Sharpe & Sortino (27.25% CBE rate, 247 trading days), Calmar ratio, Information Ratio vs EGX30, rolling 30-day Sharpe sparkline, and max drawdown details with recovery time.
